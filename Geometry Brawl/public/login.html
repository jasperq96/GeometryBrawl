Geometry Brawl

Geometry Brawl is a 2D real time PVP shooter. Each player starts with 3 lives and everytime a player gets shot, they lose a life. The game is never ending as players automatically respawn after losing their lives. Every kill gives characters experience points to upgrade and buy new cosmetic character colours and shapes in shop. Once you're ready, create an account and hop in game! Have fun!
